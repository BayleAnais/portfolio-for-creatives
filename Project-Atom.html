  • Atom Skincare

    Branding / Packaging / Web

    • Atom takes the confusion out of skincare. Featuring essential products with a clean yet energizing look, Atom invites newbies to put their best face forward, minimal effort required.

    • Recognition

    • 2023 Salazar Award for Branding

      • The brief in brief

      • Audience: Folks of all genders aged 15-25 who are unfamiliar with skincare, yet interested in getting started. They are overwhelmed with the seemingly infinite skincare options on the market, each boasting a different and equally confusing benefit.
      • USP: Beginner-friendly, no confusing language or extra steps. Makes skincare accessible and inviting through playful visuals and language for an audience with little knowledge.
      • Deliverables and goals: Create a cohesive, usable brand identity that can expand across print/digital. Develop a packaging system that compels people to try something new. Stand out in the crowded skincare market
