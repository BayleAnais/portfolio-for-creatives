  • Hue Seasonal Eating

    Branding / Print Layout Design / Packaging

    • Hue is a farm-to-table restaurant that celebrates the colourful richness of seasonal food. Using locally-sourced ingredients, Hue makes eating beautiful, delicious, and sustainable in all seasons. This project includes a menu, cookbook, and product packaging.

    • Recognition

    • 2023 Applied Arts Student Award: Design - Series (Menu)
    • 2023 Applied Arts Student Award: Editorial Design Entire Book/Magazine (Cookbook)
    • 2023 Salazar Award for Branding Honourable Mention (Full Project)

      • The Menu

      • With vibrant local food being the focal point of the brand, Hue needed a menu that captured their essence. Highlighting key colours of seasonal foods, this multi-page menu guides the customer on a colourful journey of culinary discovery. The menu features curated seasonal recipes full of exceptional flavours, and excitingly vivid colour—all from natural ingredients.
      • Featured is the Autumn menu. Menus for other seasons would be respectively colour-coded as per the seasonal colour wheel, as seen below.

      • The Cookbook

      • Familiar ingredients don’t have to be boring. The Hue cookbook livens up seasonal eating in the comfort of the home. Divided into colour-coded seasonal sections and featuring recipes for every day of the year, this cookbook is sure to brighten up even the greyest of days.

      • The Products

      • Hue brings uniquely-coloured pestos made from seasonal ingredients to the table. Vivid natural colours and seasonal flavours make even the simplest home-cooked meals feel fresh and exciting.
