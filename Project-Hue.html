  • Hue Seasonal Eating

    Branding / Print Layout Design / Packaging

    • Hue is a farm-to-table restaurant that celebrates the colourful richness of seasonal food. Using locally-sourced ingredients, Hue makes eating beautiful, delicious, and sustainable in all seasons. This project includes a menu, cookbook, and product packaging.

    • Recognition

    • 2023 Applied Arts Student Award: Design - Series (Menu)
    • 2023 Applied Arts Student Award: Editorial Design Entire Book/Magazine (Cookbook)
    • 2023 Salazar Award for Branding Honourable Mention (Full Project)

      • A well-rounded nature

      • Food grown and sourced locally is not only rich in nutrients, but boasts uniquely vibrant colours. Hue embraces these natural qualities to reimagine what it means to eat with the seasons. True to the hues of BC’s produce, this colour wheel depicts the year at a glance. It inspires Hue’s brand colours through the seasons.

      • The Cookbook

      • Familiar ingredients don’t have to be boring. The Hue cookbook is made to liven up seasonal eating in the comfort of the home. Featuring recipes for every day of the year, this cookbook is sure to brighten up even the greyest of days.
