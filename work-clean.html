  • About Me

    I am a multidisciplinary designer with a flair for picking up creative skills unusually quickly. Having taught myself numerous textile arts for the sake of learning something new, I am always on the lookout for new skills to add under my belt. I bring this knack for explorative problem-solving into my design work to transform insights into unexpected, idea-driven solutions.

    When not refining design ideas, I can be most often be found sewing, birdwatching or bouldering.
