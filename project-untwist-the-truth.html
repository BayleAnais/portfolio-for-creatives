  • Untwist The Truth

    All parts created in collaboration with Ethan Woronko and Tiffany Zhong

    • Untwist the Truth is a campaign that re-educates the public on the true history of Canada.Traditional curricula has downplayed the destructive effects of colonization on Indigenous communities. Untwist the Truth seeks to eliminate the divide between truth and teaching. Created under the guidance of Bracken Hanuse Corlett, a member of the Wuikinuxv and Klahoose Nations.
